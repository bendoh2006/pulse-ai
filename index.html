The Process

How PULSE AI Works

Five steps from symptoms to the right hospital ‚Äî in under 2 minutes.

01

Describe Your Symptoms

Enter symptoms by text, voice, or photo via the PULSE AI web app or WhatsApp ‚Äî available in French, English, Ewe, Hausa, Yoruba and more. No internet? Works offline too.

02

AI Preliminary Diagnosis

Our RAG-powered AI (RuralDiag) analyses your symptoms using a curated medical corpus. It generates a preliminary diagnosis, first-aid advice, and an urgency score in seconds.

03

Live Hospital Availability

PULSE AI queries the hospital dashboard network in real time ‚Äî checking bed occupancy, specialist schedules, and ER capacity across all registered facilities in your region.

04

Smart Routing to Best Hospital

Our SmartHosp algorithm ranks nearby hospitals by urgency match, available specialists, bed count, and distance ‚Äî then provides turn-by-turn directions to the optimal facility.

05

Follow-Up & Case History

After your visit, PULSE AI stores your consultation history, lets Lyra (our mental health AI) follow up on your wellbeing, and sends alerts to the hospital dashboard for incoming critical patients.
